FAQ

Q. Who is eligible to vote?
Ans: International students with Lamduan Email can vote the candidates .

Q.What is the total number of candidates that will ultimately be selected as finalists for the election?
Ans:We will select 5 people for following position:
1.President of the MFU International Student Club
2.Vice President in charge of International Student Activities
3.Vice President in charge of International Student Well-being
4.Secretary
5.Treasurer

Q. How many candidates can we vote?
Ans: You can vote only one candidate for each position.

Q. What if I forget to vote and over the deadline, what can I do?
Ans: You can only vote during voting time period and you cannot vote after the deadline.
